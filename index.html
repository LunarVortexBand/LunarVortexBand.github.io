Bio

Emerging from the heart of Zürich, Switzerland, Lunar Vortex is a four-piece deathcore band founded in January 2023. Bringing together a shared passion for intense music and powerful lyrics, they released their debut 2-track single, Rise Up / Cosmic Curse, in May 2024. Following that, in October 2024, they released the EP, We Wander Lost. With a growing base of dedicated listeners, the band continues to push their creative boundaries and forge their unique path in the music world.
